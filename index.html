About Us

Who We Are

A team of dedicated professionals committed to empowering individuals through mentorship, coaching, and transformative personal development.

PDI Achievers Mentorship Association is a premier professional organisation committed to providing top-notch virtual training and coaching to entrepreneurs and other professionals. Our aim is to help individuals enhance their skills, improve their performance, and achieve their goals.

We believe that every person has untapped potential waiting to be unleashed. Through structured mentorship, collaborative learning, and a supportive community, we help our members transform their aspirations into achievements.

Our Programmes →

Our Mission

To provide the knowledge, tools, and resources individuals need to achieve their personal and professional goals in a supportive and collaborative learning environment.

Our Vision

To empower individuals to reach their full potential by providing world-class personal development training and coaching that inspires growth, creativity and innovation.

What We Offer

Our Programmes

Comprehensive mentorship and development programmes designed to unlock your potential at every stage of your personal and professional journey.

Personal Mentorship

One-on-one guidance for personal development

Our personal mentorship programme pairs you with experienced mentors who provide individualised guidance tailored to your specific goals and challenges. Through regular sessions, you'll develop essential life skills, build confidence, and create actionable plans for personal growth.

Business Clinic

Strategic business guidance and solutions

Join our business clinic to receive expert advice on entrepreneurship, business strategy, and professional development. Our experienced business mentors provide practical insights, help you navigate challenges, and support your journey to business success.

Career Guidance

Professional career development support

Navigate your career path with confidence through our comprehensive career guidance programme. We offer resume building, interview preparation, career transition support, and professional skill development to help you achieve your career aspirations.

Networking

Building meaningful professional connections

Expand your professional network through our organised networking events, workshops, and social gatherings. Connect with like-minded individuals, industry professionals, and potential collaborators in a supportive environment.

Member Sensitization

Awareness and education programmes

Stay informed and engaged through our member sensitization programmes. We provide educational workshops, awareness sessions, and training programmes on relevant topics to keep our community informed and empowered.

Industry Capacity Building

Enhancing skills for industry professionals

Our Industry Capacity Building programme equips professionals with up-to-date skills, knowledge, and best practices relevant to their sectors. Through workshops, seminars, and expert-led sessions, participants gain practical insights to boost productivity and innovation in their industries.

Young Managers Mentorship

Guidance for emerging leaders and managers

The Young Managers Mentorship programme supports aspiring and newly appointed managers with mentorship from experienced leaders. Participants develop leadership, decision-making, and people management skills to excel in their managerial roles.
